A brief bit about me...

I've been a stay-at-home mom for the last 7 years. Before that I did the following for work:

  • taught environmental education in California, Oregon, Montana, and Arizona
  • worked as a park ranger at Lake Powell and Capitol Reef National Parks
  • researched building materials for an environmentally sustainable demonstration house in Utah which no longer exists
  • went to truck driving school in New Mexico and drove an asphalt truck for 3 weeks but was fired when I ran over a broom handle
  • worked at Mount Bachelor so that I could snowboard every day
  • made espresso at the first Starbucks in Bend, OR
  • took 911 calls as an EMS dispatcher
  • tried to sell Kirby vacuums for 1 week
  • went to law school in New Mexico to study environmental and American Indian law
  • worked in a small cubicle writing hazardous waste regulations for the Wisconsin Department of Natural Resources (the worst job I've ever had, other than selling vacuums and driving a truck. oh, and driving a taxi.)
  • wrote environmental policy at a small nonprofit for state legislators
  • practiced immigration law in a small firm while being harassed by an abusive boss
  • drove drunk people around in a taxi for 2 months
  • ran for city council in Madison and lost
  • worked as director of an animal protection nonprofit
  • opened a real estate brokerage and law practice in Wisconsin
