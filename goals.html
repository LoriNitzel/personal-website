My Career Goals

I have no desire to make a bunch of money and I don't need to work at a cutting-edge company. My life goal is to buy a farm in either the US or England, grow lots of veggies and fruits, and rescue a few more animals. I need a job that allows me to bring in a decent salary while I pursue this dream.
